# Audio Assets

**Coloque os arquivos MP3 nesta pasta para habilitar o áudio da revelação.** O app funciona sem eles (fallback silencioso), mas a experiência fica mais imersiva com som.

## Arquivos necessários

### Cell/Box Sounds
- `cell_land.mp3` - Heavy metallic impact when the containment cell lands

### Crack Sounds (3 variations for variety)
- `crack_1.mp3` - Metal cracking/fracturing sound
- `crack_2.mp3` - Metal cracking/fracturing sound (variation)
- `crack_3.mp3` - Metal cracking/fracturing sound (variation)

### Build-up Sounds
- `energy_buildup.mp3` - Rising energy/tension sound (1.5-2 seconds)

### Shatter Sound
- `shatter.mp3` - Glass/metal shattering explosion

### Reveal Fanfares (one per rarity)
- `reveal_common.mp3` - Simple reveal jingle
- `reveal_uncommon.mp3` - Slightly more elaborate jingle
- `reveal_rare.mp3` - More impressive jingle
- `reveal_epic.mp3` - Epic reveal fanfare
- `reveal_legendary.mp3` - Legendary reveal fanfare with full orchestration

## Recommended Specs
- Format: MP3 (for browser compatibility)
- Sample Rate: 44.1kHz
- Bit Rate: 128-192 kbps
- Duration: 
  - Short effects: 0.5-1.5 seconds
  - Fanfares: 2-4 seconds
  - Energy buildup: 1.5-2 seconds
